Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 11, 2026

FileDrop ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our peer-to-peer file transfer application.

Information We Collect

FileDrop is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information:

  • No Personal Information: We do not collect, store, or process any personal information such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers.
  • No File Content: Files you transfer are sent directly between devices using WebRTC peer-to-peer technology. We never store, access, or view your files.
  • Session Codes: Temporary session codes are generated for establishing connections. These are stored temporarily on our signaling server (Internet Computer blockchain) and automatically expire after 10 minutes.
  • WebRTC Signaling Data: Connection metadata (SDP offers/answers and ICE candidates) is temporarily stored to facilitate peer-to-peer connections. This data contains no file content or personal information.

How We Use Information

The limited technical data we handle is used solely for:

  • Establishing direct peer-to-peer connections between your devices
  • Facilitating WebRTC signaling to enable file transfers
  • Maintaining session state during active transfers

Data Storage and Security

  • Temporary Storage: Session data is stored temporarily on the Internet Computer blockchain and automatically expires after 10 minutes.
  • No File Storage: Files are transferred directly between devices. We never store files on our servers.
  • Encryption: All file transfers use WebRTC's built-in encryption (DTLS-SRTP).
  • No Third-Party Analytics: We do not use third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

Camera Permission

FileDrop requests camera permission to enable QR code scanning for easy session joining. Camera access is:

  • Only used when you explicitly choose to scan a QR code
  • Processed locally on your device
  • Never recorded, stored, or transmitted to our servers
  • Optional - you can always enter session codes manually instead

Internet Identity

FileDrop uses Internet Identity for authentication. Internet Identity:

  • Is a privacy-preserving authentication system
  • Does not share your personal information with FileDrop
  • Provides anonymous authentication using cryptographic keys
  • Learn more at identity.ic0.app

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or rent any data to third parties. Period.

Children's Privacy

FileDrop does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is designed for general audiences and does not target children.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect or store personal information, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. Session data automatically expires after 10 minutes.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

Open Source

FileDrop is built on open-source technology. You can review our code and verify our privacy practices.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the app's support channels or visit our website.
